Prem is a powerful AI platform designed to simplify the creation and deployment of your own AI models. It features top-tier pre-trained models and provides the capability to fine-tune and distill them effortlessly with our autonomous fine-tuning agent.

We designed Prem to make AI integration seamless, efficient, and effective, so you focus on developing your product and not all the AI complexity.

### Heres what you can do with Prem:

- Access the best in class AI models without any vendor lock-in.
- Create your own fine-tuned AI models with ease.
- Distill models into smaller, more efficient models.
- Use our [SDKs](/get-started/sdk) to integrate Prem into your own codebase.
- Securely integrate your documents into your models without having to build your own RAG.
- Bring your own [Qdrant](https://qdrant.tech/) vector database.
- Fine-tune your models with our autonomous fine-tuning agent.
- Deploy your models to your own local infrastructure.

Check out our [Quickstart Guide](/introduction) to get started in minutes.
